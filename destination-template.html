    RELEVANT NOTICES

    Resort Refurbishments
    - Please note that we are currently busy with unit renovations and this may cause some noise disturbances depending on the location of your unit. Noise will only start form 10AM in consideration of our guests.

    Check In/Out
    Friday and Monday
    Check-in 16:00
    Check-out 10:00

    Charges
    There is no key / breakage / appliance deposit for your convenience - however, we do ask that breakages are reported and paid timeously at reception. We do charge per hour for any late departures. The chalets are strictly non-smoking and a penalty fee will be payable.

    Please note
    - Load shedding does affect the resort and the water supply. It is advised to bring extra rechargeable lights and anything else you might need during outages. The resort does supply candles and matches. We suggest that you download the ESP application and select Sedgefield as a location for the most accurate schedule and updates on load shedding in our area.
    - The keys are to be collected at Baywater Village, N2, Swartvlei, Sedgefield.
    - No over occupancy will be allowed.
    - Baywater Village makes use of borehole water and it is advisable that guests bring along their own drinking water. Drinking water can also be purchased from the Kiosk onsite.
